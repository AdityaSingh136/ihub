Data Driven Drug Discovery (D4)

PLAS - 5K

PLAS-5K dataset is a collection of images related to plastic pollution and recycling efforts. It consists of images captured from various locations around the world, highlighting the impact of plastic waste on the environment and showcasing initiatives to address this issue.

PLAS-5K Variation 2

PLAS-5K Variation 2 dataset focuses on studying the evolution of plastic waste and its impact on ecosystems over time. It includes images captured through different seasons and years, providing valuable insights into the changing patterns of plastic pollution and environmental responses.

APO-Bind

APO-Bind dataset is a comprehensive collection of molecular binding data for various proteins and ligands. It contains information about binding affinities, binding sites, and interaction energies, enabling researchers to study protein-ligand interactions and aid in drug design and discovery.

Plas5k Variation3

Plas5k Variation3 dataset is an extension of the PLAS-5K dataset, focusing on variations in plastic waste composition and types across different geographic regions. It offers a detailed view of plastic waste characteristics and distribution, aiding in understanding the global plastic pollution crisis.
